Who Are We?


At Stevens Institute of Technology, the IEEE Student Branch gives students both conceptual and hands-on exposure to fields such as electrical engineering and computer engineering. All our events are beginner-friendly and open to anyone who wants to learn. We focus on helping you build real skills, explore new interests, and be part of a strong, supportive engineering community. Our workshops and activities include soldering, PCB design, coding sessions, guest speaker talks, and social events like bowling.



Mission Statement


Our mission is to foster a supportive and inclusive community for all students, with an emphasis on electrical and computer engineering majors. We aim to introduce students to core engineering concepts and give them opportunities to build skills they will use throughout their time at Stevens Institute of Technology and in their careers.
