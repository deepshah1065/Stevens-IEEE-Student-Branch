Welcome to the Stevens Institute of Technology Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers Student Chapter!


The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Student Chapter at Stevens Institute of Technology provides students with opportunities to explore and apply concepts in electrical and computer engineering. The organization fosters technical growth, collaboration, and professional development through events, projects, and mentorship from upperclassmen.
