IEEE North Jersey Section MTT-Society and AP-Society Joint Chapter


The IEEE North Jersey Section MTT-Society and AP-Society Joint Chapter is hosting its 39th Annual Symposium and Mini-Show. The event focuses on selected topics in Radio Frequency (RF) and microwave technologies for both commercial and military applications.

It will take place on Thursday, October 9th, 2025, at 16 Eagle Rock Ave., East Hanover, NJ 07936. For any questions, you can contact (973)-992-7425. The event runs from 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM.

There will be 10-12 lectures from leading companies, emphasizing military electronics, wireless technologies, and microwave communications. The mini-show will feature approximately 30–40 exhibitors, and a complimentary lunch will be provided.

Transportation will be available from Stevens Institute of Technology. The QR code to register is included below.




IEEE AP-S Stevens Chapter Inauguration


Stevens Institute of Technology is launching its new chapter of the IEEE Antennas and Propagation Society (AP-S), with the inauguration event on October 11th from 9:00 am to 3:30 pm in Burchard 111. The event will feature presentations by professional IEEE leaders from around the world, a student poster session, and the formal chapter inauguration. Students are encouraged to RSVP by October 1st using the QR code on the attached flyer. Food will be provided.

The IEEE AP-S is a global professional organization dedicated to advancing theory and concepts in antennas, propagation, and electromagnetics to benefit humanity. It also supports research and innovation in related areas such as wireless communication, radar, remote sensing, and satellite technologies. As of August 19, 2025, the Society has reached a record 14,000 members, the highest in its history.
